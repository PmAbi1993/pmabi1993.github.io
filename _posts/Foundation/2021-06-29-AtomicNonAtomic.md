# Overview

The properties of variables written in obj-c can be classified as Atomic and Non-Atomic properties.

Properties in swift are `Non-Atomic` by default. 

```objective-c
@property (atomic, strong) NSString *atomicProperty;
@property (nonatomic, strong) NSString *nonAtomicProperty;
```
